# Efeonce legal, privacy, and compliance routing

Do not provide a durable legal digest from this architecture skill.

When a decision depends on privacy, employment, financial, IP, AI, data-transfer, retention, breach, residency, or sector obligations:

1. Load `legal-privacy-ip-operator` and its current source modules.
2. Identify jurisdictions, data categories, subjects, purposes, processors/controllers, transfers, retention, and decision owner.
3. Verify primary official sources as of the decision date.
4. Separate legal requirements from contractual commitments, certification targets, risk preferences, and architecture controls.
5. Require Legal/Privacy review when interpretation affects design, notices, rights, contracts, or launch authority.
6. Record uncertainty and effective dates. Never claim compliance from a checklist or architecture document alone.

For Greenhouse, also load the domain's security/access/privacy architecture and the active task or ADR. Legal sources and conclusions belong to the legal skill and canonical legal artifacts, not this overlay.
