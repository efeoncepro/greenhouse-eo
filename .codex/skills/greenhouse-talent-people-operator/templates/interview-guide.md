# Structured Interview Guide template

One guide per role, same for every candidate. Structured interviews are the strongest predictor (Sackett et al. 2022). Untrained + unstructured = low validity + bias.

## Debrief

- Each interviewer submits ratings + evidence **before** discussion (avoid anchoring/groupthink).
- Compare against the scorecard; decide on a diverse slate + evidence, not gut.
- Note bias risks observed (halo/horns, similarity, recency).

## Output = advisory recommendation

- Per-competency scores + evidence + confidence.
- **Never** a "hire/reject" verdict — the hiring manager decides (AI-Act human oversight; Greenhouse invariant).
