# Territory selection

Scoring uses a 0–5 scale. The anchor must communicate both premium craft and multiplication of one creative direction across many assets.

| Territory | Concept | Brand fit | Hierarchy | Scales to formats | Originality | Craft | Total / 30 |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| T01 — Iridescent precision | 4.0 | 4.6 | 4.5 | 3.5 | 4.0 | 4.6 | 25.2 |
| T02 — Chromatic wake | 4.8 | 4.4 | 4.3 | 4.6 | 4.7 | 4.2 | **27.0** |
| T03 — Modular orbit | 4.4 | 4.3 | 4.1 | 4.8 | 3.8 | 4.1 | 25.5 |

## Decision

Select **T02 — Chromatic wake**. It makes the campaign promise visible without explaining it: one precise hummingbird produces a disciplined field of chromatic fragments, turning a single idea into a system of outputs. T01 is the strongest beauty image but says less about scale. T03 is literal and adaptable, but its glass frames drift toward generic interface imagery.

## Development note

The lower-left wake competes with copy. Seedream Pro will preserve the single-bird identity and scaling metaphor while consolidating the wake toward the center/right and reserving a calmer upper-left field. Copy and branding remain deterministic; the model must not generate either.
