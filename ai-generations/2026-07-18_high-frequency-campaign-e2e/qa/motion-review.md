# Motion review

## Release set

- 15 s brand-light hero · 9:16;
- 15 s brand-light hero · 16:9;
- 10 s brand-light master · 9:16;
- 10 s brand-light master · 16:9;
- 6 s brand-light bumper · 9:16;
- 6 s brand-light bumper · 16:9.

The 3 s Omni output is a technical endpoint probe. It is intentionally excluded from the release set.

## Visual review

- The bird remains a single subject across both masters; no cloned heads, torsos or tails were observed in
  the 1 fps contact sheets.
- The modular wake accelerates and changes density/color without losing the campaign's core visual grammar.
- Vertical motion creates a strong upward trajectory suited to full-screen social.
- Landscape motion preserves a wide directional trail and resolves cleanly to the 16:9 end card.
- Both masters use eight seconds of generative motion plus a two-second deterministic branded resolution.
- The six-second bumpers use four seconds of motion plus the same two-second end card.
- The 15-second heroes use one approved generative shot, two exact claim beats, a deterministic wall of
  real campaign formats and a 2.5-second end card. No new inference was required.
- The format wall makes the scalability proposition visible instead of relying only on copy.
- Logo, headline, support and URL remain exact because the end card is composed from the approved still system.

## Audio status

AAC audio is present and loudness/peak were measured in all six outputs. The 15-second heroes measure
`−16.3/−16.4 LUFS` and `−2.0/−2.2 dBFS` after compressor + loudness normalization. Masters measure
`−20.4 LUFS` (16:9) and `−30.7 LUFS` (9:16); bumpers measure `−19.0 LUFS` (16:9) and `−30.6 LUFS`
(9:16). Those four are measurement-only reference mixes, not target-normalized channel masters.
There is no dialogue by contract. Codec/presence/duration and measurement are technically verified; a human
listening pass on headphones, phone speaker and muted autoplay context remains mandatory before paid activation.
Captions are not required for dialogue, but platform metadata and a no-audio comprehension check remain required.

## Routing and fallback

Seedance reference-to-video was the explicit contingency if Omni failed identity, anatomy, set or temporal
continuity. It was not invoked because both Omni clean masters passed review. The 3 s probe remains outside the
release and its USD 0.390 cost is excluded from the USD 2.965 release path. Both 15 s heroes reuse approved
material and add USD 0 of model inference.

## Verdict

**PASS for creative release.** Motion is now a 15/10/6-second campaign family, not a three-second demonstration.
Media launch remains pending human audio listen, channel normalization of masters/bumpers if trafficked,
legal/brand approval, trafficking and performance design.
